What To Do If Your Driver Canceled

Last updated: May 2026 · Content Owner: Rider Support Team

Quick summary: If your driver canceled, the Uber app will automatically search for a new driver. You should not be charged a cancellation fee. If you were charged, you can dispute it directly in the app.

What Happens After a Driver Cancels

  1. You will receive a notification in the Uber app that your driver has canceled.
  2. The app will automatically begin searching for a new driver.
  3. If no driver is available, you may need to request a new ride manually.
  4. Check your app to confirm whether a new driver has been assigned.

Were You Charged a Cancellation Fee?

Use the guide below to find out if the charge is valid or if you should request a refund.

🟡 If your driver canceled while on the way to pick you up:

You should not be charged a cancellation fee. The app should automatically find you a new driver. If you were charged, dispute the fee.

🔴 If your driver canceled after waiting at your pickup location:

A cancellation fee may apply depending on your ride type and how long the driver waited:

Ride Type Driver Wait Time Before Fee Applies
UberX, UberXL, Comfort, Pet 5 minutes
Comfort 10 minutes
Uber Black, SUV 15 minutes
UberX Share 2 minutes

🟢 You should NOT be charged if:

  • Your driver had not made progress toward your pickup location
  • Your driver was running late
  • Your driver was still completing another trip

How To Dispute a Cancellation Fee

  1. Open the Uber app and tap Account
  2. Tap Trips and select the trip in question
  3. Tap Get Trip Help
  4. Select I was charged a cancellation fee
  5. Follow the prompts to submit your dispute
