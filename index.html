  <!-- QUICK SUMMARY -->

  <div style="background-color: #f5f5f5; border-left: 4px solid #000000; padding: 16px 20px; margin-bottom: 32px; border-radius: 4px;">

    <p style="font-size: 15px; margin: 0; line-height: 1.6;">

      <strong>Quick summary:</strong> If your driver canceled, the Uber app will automatically search for a new driver. You should not be charged a cancellation fee. If you were charged, you can dispute it directly in the app.

    </p>

  </div>

  <!-- SECTION 1: WHAT HAPPENS NEXT -->

  <h2 style="font-size: 20px; font-weight: bold; border-bottom: 1px solid #ddd; padding-bottom: 8px; margin-bottom: 16px;">What Happens After a Driver Cancels</h2>

  <ol style="font-size: 15px; line-height: 1.8; padding-left: 20px; margin-bottom: 32px;">

    <li>You will receive a notification in the Uber app that your driver has canceled.</li>

    <li>The app will automatically begin searching for a new driver.</li>

    <li>If no driver is available, you may need to request a new ride manually.</li>

    <li>Check your app to confirm whether a new driver has been assigned.</li>

  </ol>

  <!-- SECTION 2: DECISION TREE -->

  <h2 style="font-size: 20px; font-weight: bold; border-bottom: 1px solid #ddd; padding-bottom: 8px; margin-bottom: 16px;">Were You Charged a Cancellation Fee?</h2>

  <p style="font-size: 15px; margin-bottom: 16px;">Use the guide below to find out if the charge is valid or if you should request a refund.</p>

  <!-- Decision Branch 1 -->

  <div style="background-color: #fff8e1; border: 1px solid #f0c040; border-radius: 6px; padding: 16px 20px; margin-bottom: 16px;">

    <p style="font-weight: bold; font-size: 15px; margin: 0 0 8px 0;">🟡 If your driver canceled while on the way to pick you up:</p>

    <p style="font-size: 15px; margin: 0; line-height: 1.6;">You should <strong>not</strong> be charged a cancellation fee. The app should automatically find you a new driver. If you were charged, <a href="#dispute" style="color: #000; font-weight: bold;">dispute the fee</a>.</p>

  </div>

  <!-- Decision Branch 3 -->

    <p style="font-weight: bold; font-size: 15px; margin: 0 0 8px 0;">🟢 You should NOT be charged if:</p>

    <ul style="font-size: 15px; margin: 0; padding-left: 20px; line-height: 1.8;">

      <li>Your driver had not made progress toward your pickup location</li>

      <li>Your driver was running late</li>

      <li>Your driver was still completing another trip</li>

    </ul>

  <!-- SECTION 3: HOW TO DISPUTE -->

  <h2 id="dispute" style="font-size: 20px; font-weight: bold; border-bottom: 1px solid #ddd; padding-bottom: 8px; margin-bottom: 16px;">How To Dispute a Cancellation Fee</h2>

  <ol style="font-size: 15px; line-height: 1.8; padding-left: 20px; margin-bottom: 32px;">

    <li>Open the <strong>Uber app</strong> and tap <strong>Account</strong></li>

    <li>Tap <strong>Trips</strong> and select the trip in question</li>

    <li>Tap <strong>Get Trip Help</strong></li>

    <li>Select <strong>I was charged a cancellation fee</strong></li>

    <li>Follow the prompts to submit your dispute</li>

  </ol>
